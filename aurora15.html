AURORA IDOL


For all the music lovers! A Big treat for you!
Aurora 2013 presents Aurora Idol in association with “Shankar Mahadevan Academy”, an ultimate singing talent hunt.
Prizes: Worth 25000/- & chance to sing with Shankar Mahadevan
Interested participants can register for Aurora 2013 Idol through Shankar Mahadevan Academy. All the registered participant will get a detailed score sheet and comprehensive feedback (Audio as well as written) within 36 hours.
Selected top scorers will be called for the main event (finale) in ABV-IIITM Gwalior’s cultural fest ‘Aurora 2013’.

HIGHLIGHTS:


ENTRY FEE:INR 300


Audition Start: Feb 11th 2014


Audition Ends: Feb 20th 2014


Audition Result:Feb 22nd 2014


Songs will be assessed on 7 parameters & scores awarded from 100 points
